**Summary**: Tentative flavor for [[sage-magic|Sage]]: tied to a mushroom-folk ancestry and a cordyceps-style zombie plague as a societal threat. Far less developed than [[splendor-magic|Splendor]] and [[codex-magic|Codex]], the only domain with anything decided among the other seven as of 2026-07-12.

---

## What's actually decided

Unlike Splendor and Codex, this isn't yet a description of how Sage casting itself feels distinct, it's an ancestry and a threat, tentatively associated with the domain:

- A mushroom-folk ancestry, tied to Sage's existing domain flavor (druids, rangers, nature).
- A cordyceps-style transformation into zombie-like creatures, modeled on *The Last of Us*'s Clickers: infected humans turned into something no longer recognizably themselves.
- The intended threat level: comparable to *A Song of Ice and Fire*'s White Walkers, a real, existential danger to a medieval society, not a low-level wilderness encounter.
