---
title: Holy Relics
---

**Summary**: Relics from the Divine Age, usable only by those with holy blood ([[miracles|Miracles]]). Hereditary, immensely powerful, and fought over across generations.

---

## The core idea

Relics from the Divine Age's war against [[the-ones-below|the ones below]] can only be safely wielded by someone with holy blood, a [[miracles|Miracle]].

## Consequence for unworthy use

Wielding a Relic without the matching Miracle is perilous: the unworthy wielder is said to be cursed, twisted by a power their blood was never meant to hold. What exactly the curse does to them, the old stories don't agree on, and few who've seen it have lived to be specific.

## Hereditary, and fought over

These Relics are extraordinarily powerful, passed down within families rather than found or crafted freely, and a real driver of war and politics in their own right: houses and nations fight over who holds which Relic, not just what it does in a single fight.

## A specific example: the Voldis armor

[[voldaen|House Voldis's]] own hereditary Relic is a suit of armor as strong as it is ancient. It was lost when [[edrion-voldis|Edrion Voldis]] fell fighting [[the-ophanim|the Ophanim]] in 331 VR, and has not been recovered since.
