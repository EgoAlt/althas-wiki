---
title: "Holy Relics"
---

**Summary**: Relics from the Divine Age, usable only by those with holy blood ([[miracles|Miracles]]). Misuse without the matching Miracle risks transforming the wielder into a monster. Hereditary, extremely powerful, and fought over across generations.

---

## The core idea

Relics from the Divine Age's war against [[the-ones-below|the ones below]] can only be safely wielded by someone with holy blood, a [[miracles|Miracle]]. A Relic paired with its matching Miracle works as intended; without it, using the Relic is dangerous.
