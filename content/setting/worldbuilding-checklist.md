---
title: Worldbuilding Checklist
---

**Summary**: A running checklist of parts of Althas still open for the table to help shape, from the names of the months to the songs people sing.

---

Daggerheart invites players into the worldbuilding, and much of Althas is still unwritten. This page lists the open pieces: concrete gaps anyone at the table can help fill. Pick whatever draws you and bring an idea, and it can become part of the wiki. Some corners of Althas are kept back to be revealed in play; everything here is fair game.

## Faith, calendar, and time

- [ ] Name the ten months of the [[calendar|Calendar]] year.
- [ ] Name the eleven days of the week (they currently carry only roles: weekend, prayer, work).
- [ ] Name the three Closing Holidays that end the year and mark the final battle of the Divine Age.
- [ ] Describe how people mark the three Closing Holidays: what is done, eaten, mourned, or celebrated.
- [ ] Name the thirty Saints of the Divine Age, one for each prayer day of the year: who each was and what they are patron of.
- [ ] Set out the [[the-holy-see|Parish of Orthodoxy]]'s rules for prayer and conduct in church, its common rites (naming, confession, marriage, funeral), and the everyday beliefs and superstitions of the faithful.
- [ ] Write common prayers, blessings, and hymns of the faith of [[the-one-above|the One Above]].

## The Church

- [ ] Describe what ordinary people visit the everyday Parishes for: Finance, Resource, and Cartography, and what a commoner actually needs from each (a seal, a witnessed contract, a map, a tax assessment).
- [ ] Name holy places and pilgrimage sites across Althas beyond [[hilltop|Hilltop]], and religious houses beyond the [[convent-of-saint-trefan|Convent of Saint Trefan]].

## Peoples

- [ ] Flesh out [[faeries|Faerie]] message-carrier culture: how a courier is hired and trusted, their etiquette, and the folklore around them.
- [ ] Describe [[infernis|Infernis]] caravan life: the goods, news, and rumors they carry, their music and customs, and why they are feared.
- [ ] Describe the working-class culture of the [[giants|Giants]] in [[polaris|Polaris]]: their labor, their songs, their community.
- [ ] Which other ancestries of Althas deserve a fuller place in the world, and how would you expand them (their history, communities, and daily life)?

## Places and trade

- [ ] Name the capital cities or principal seats of [[voldaen|Voldaen]], [[polaris|Polaris]], [[armada|Armada]], and [[jesthaen|Jesthaen]].
- [ ] Name the currency and coinage used across Althas, and the goods most traded.
- [ ] Name notable towns, ports, and landmarks beyond Hilltop and [[drinmery|Drinmery]].
- [ ] Describe the banner and heraldry of each of the five nations.

## Legends and songs

- [ ] Add folk legends and tavern tales told across Althas (the [[kingslayer|Kingslayer]] is already one).
- [ ] Write songs and ballads of the recent war and of the Divine Age.
- [ ] What do common people believe became of [[the-one-above|the One Above]] after he departed?
