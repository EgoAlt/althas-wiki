**Summary**: The founding era of Althas, when [[the-one-above|the One Above]] fought and cast down [[the-ones-below|the Ones Below]], sealing them away. Its close crowned the first God King, founded [[voldaen|Voldaen]], and set year 0 of the Voldaen Reckoning.

---

In the Divine Age, [[the-one-above|the One Above]] protected the mortal creations of Althas and warred against [[the-ones-below|the Ones Below]], the primordial powers of chaos. The war ended when the One Above cast the Ones Below down and sealed them away; the blow that ended it left the crater that became [[crater-lake|the Crater Lake]] at the center of the continent.

With victory secured, the people of Althas crowned their first God King and founded the kingdom of [[voldaen|Voldaen]] beneath him. The One Above set his seat at what is now [[hilltop|Hilltop]], then departed the world. The years since are counted from that founding, in the Voldaen Reckoning: the close of the Divine Age is year 0 VR. See the [[timeline|Timeline]] for the chronology that follows.
