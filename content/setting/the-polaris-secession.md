**Summary**: The peaceful partition of around 241 VR by which the scholars and mages of the north broke away from [[voldaen|Voldaen]] to found [[polaris|Polaris]], settled by treaty rather than the war that had scarred the continent a century earlier.

---

By the middle of the third century VR, the northern academies and their mages had grown into a power of their own, chafing under the crown of [[house-voldis|House Voldis]]. Rather than repeat the bloodshed of [[the-mad-kings-war|the Mad King's War]], crown and north settled their split by negotiated partition. Out of it came [[polaris|Polaris]], an independent magocracy ruled by a Triumvirate of Archmages. As part of the settlement, a younger prince of the line renounced his own claim and afterward passed out of the record.
