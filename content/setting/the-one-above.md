title: The One Above
---

**Summary**: The god of Splendor, worshipped by [[the-holy-see|the Holy See]] and [[canton-of-inquisition|the Canton of Inquisition]]. Fought a war against other gods, [[the-ones-below|the ones below]], during the Divine Age; granted [[miracles|Miracles]] to allies during that war. Still alive, but has since left Althas.

---

Worshipped throughout Althas as the source of [[splendor-magic|Splendor magic]] (see that page for how incantation-based magic works as a favor granted rather than a caster's own power) and of [[miracles|Miracles]], the hereditary gifts bestowed on allies during the Divine Age's war against [[the-ones-below|the ones below]].

The One Above is still alive. He has left Althas, for reasons not yet established.
