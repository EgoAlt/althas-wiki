---
title: Eltanin
---

**Summary**: Current leader of [[draconis|Draconis]], having inherited the role from his father [[thuban|Thuban]] at 17. Older brother of [[rastaban|Rastaban]]; publicly denies knowing him.

---

"Elt" for short. Where his younger brother [[rastaban|Rastaban]] wanted to see the rest of the world, Eltanin was exactly like their father [[thuban|Thuban]]: stoic, strong, calm, and fully accepting of Draconis's survival-first philosophy. He was groomed to lead from the start, and took over as Thuban retired when both brothers turned 17, the same year Rastaban left for [[polaris|Polaris]].

Unlike Rastaban, who carved sacred texts into his own skin and has a rare three-eyed Strange Pattern, Eltanin has the ordinary single eye of a giant. When asked about his brother, Eltanin, with a straight face, claims to have never heard of such a being, protecting Draconis from the association rather than acknowledging Ras at all.
