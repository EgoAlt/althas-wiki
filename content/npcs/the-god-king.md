**Summary**: The founder and first king of [[voldaen|Voldaen]], crowned at the close of the Divine Age. [[house-voldis|House Voldis]] rules by claimed divine descent from him. Remembered only by his title.

---

When [[the-one-above|the One Above]] cast down [[the-ones-below|the Ones Below]] and ended the Divine Age, the people of Althas crowned their first God King and founded [[voldaen|Voldaen]] beneath him. Every monarch of [[house-voldis|House Voldis]] since claims rule by divine descent from him, and holds the crown as a sacred inheritance rather than a worldly one.

The histories keep no personal name for him, only the title. By the tellings of the faith, he passed from the world alongside the One Above himself when the god departed Althas, his work of founding done.
