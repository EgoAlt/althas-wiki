**Summary**: A king of [[voldaen|Voldaen]] (r. 93-117 VR) who proclaimed himself the One Above returned, and whose heresy plunged the realm into civil war between crown and church.

---

Generations after the founding, a king of [[house-voldis|House Voldis]] declared before the realm that he was [[the-one-above|the One Above]] returned to Althas in the flesh. The [[the-holy-see|Holy See]], whose whole authority rested on serving an absent god, named the claim heresy at once. Crown and church fell into open war, the first great fracturing of a continent that had flown under one banner since the Divine Age.

He is remembered by no other name than the Mad King. The war he began ended only with his death at the hands of his own son and successor, [[the-blessed-king|the Blessed King]].
