**Summary**: King of [[voldaen|Voldaen]], father of [[edrion-voldis|Edrion Voldis]]. Assassinated in 334 VR, shortly after his son's death fighting [[the-ophanim|the Ophanim]], leaving a child heir on the throne.

---

King of Voldaen at the time [[the-ophanim|the Ophanim]] descended on [[hilltop|Hilltop]]. When his son and heir, [[edrion-voldis|Edrion Voldis]], died fighting it as one of the Five Heroes in 334 VR, Valthis was assassinated soon after. The murder left Edrion's young daughter [[valis-voldis|Valis Voldis]] the last heir of the direct line before she was old enough to rule, opening the door to the noble coup that shaped the rest of the Voldis Succession Crisis. Valthis was also father, by a common woman, to the baseborn [[aldric-voldis|Aldric Voldis]].

His killer, still called "the Kingslayer" in Voldaen taverns, has never been identified; see [[kingslayer|the Kingslayer]] for the tavern legend.
