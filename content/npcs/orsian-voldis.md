---
title: Orsian Voldis
---

**Summary**: Child-king of [[voldaen|Voldaen]] after his grandfather's assassination, ruled as a puppet of the noble coalition that exiled his regent. Died heirless in 348 VR, triggering the crisis that birthed [[jesthaen|Jesthaen]].

---

Son of [[edrion-voldis|Edrion Voldis]], grandson of [[valthis-voldis|Valthis Voldis]]. Inherited the throne of Voldaen as a sickly child after Valthis's assassination in 331 VR, too young to rule in his own name. His father's will had named his uncle [[aldric-voldis|Aldric Voldis]] regent for exactly this situation, but within a few years the capital's oldest noble families staged a quiet coup, stripped Aldric of authority, and exiled him to the western borders under the pretense of military necessity.

From then on Orsian was, in the wiki's own words, "little more than a royal puppet," surrounded by the coalition of nobles who ruled in his name. He died without heirs in 348 VR. The capital houses backed his elder sister [[valis-voldis|Valis Voldis]] for unbroken succession; the southern nobles answered with a rival claim for Aldric's return. That fracture became the revolution that produced [[jesthaen|Jesthaen]].
