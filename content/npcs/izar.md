---
title: Izar
---

**Summary**: Young mage, son of [[hesper|Hesper]], who repeatedly challenged his mother for her seat on Polaris's Triumvirate of Archmages.

---

Challenged his mother, [[hesper|Hesper]], for her Triumvirate seat again and again, losing every time. Polaris society mostly read the ritual as Izar doing an old, stubborn woman a mercy by giving her something to win at, since she always refused to actually kill him.

The last challenge was different: Izar arrived unnaturally powerful and fast, changed by something unexplained (possibly involving his wand). Hesper vanished in the fight's aftermath, her house left in shambles. Izar told anyone who'd listen that a "horrible giant" had invaded and murdered an archmage, and that he himself had struck the invader down.
