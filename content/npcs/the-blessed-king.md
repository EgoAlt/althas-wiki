**Summary**: The king of [[voldaen|Voldaen]] (r. 117-149 VR) who ended his father's heretical war by slaying [[the-mad-king|the Mad King]] and won [[hilltop|Hilltop]] its independence. Remembered by the faithful as blessed for it.

---

Son of [[the-mad-king|the Mad King]], he ended the civil war his father had started by striking the man down himself and denouncing his heresy before the faithful. For turning against a blasphemer king, even his own father, the [[the-holy-see|Holy See]] and the common people named him the Blessed King, and the name outlasted whatever he was called at birth.

Out of the settlement that ended the war, [[hilltop|Hilltop]], long the seat of the [[the-holy-see|Holy See]], passed fully into the Church's keeping as a sovereign nation rather than a holding of the Voldis crown. The [[house-voldis|House Voldis]] line continued through him.
