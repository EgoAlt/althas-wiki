**Summary**: A common woman of Voldaen, mother of [[aldric-voldis|Aldric Voldis]] by King [[valthis-voldis|Valthis Voldis]], and the namesake of the [[jesthaen|Jesthaen Republic]].

---

Jestha was a commoner of [[voldaen|Voldaen]] and the mother of [[aldric-voldis|Aldric Voldis]], the bastard son King [[valthis-voldis|Valthis Voldis]] never legitimized. It was her common blood that marked Aldric as unfit in the eyes of the capital's noble houses, and her name he gave to the republic he raised in their despite: [[jesthaen|Jesthaen]], the nation built by and for the people she came from.
