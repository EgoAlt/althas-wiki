**Summary**: Bastard half-brother of [[edrion-voldis|Edrion Voldis]] and one of the Five Heroes who fought [[the-ophanim|the Ophanim]]. Named regent, then exiled by a noble coup; his return became the rallying cry of the southern nobles during the succession crisis.

---

Bastard half-brother of High Prince [[edrion-voldis|Edrion Voldis]]. Fought alongside him as one of the Five Heroes against [[the-ophanim|the Ophanim]] in 331 VR and survived, respected by soldiers and border lords for it, but distrusted by the capital's oldest noble families, especially after speaking openly of reform.

Edrion's will named Aldric regent should [[valthis-voldis|Valthis Voldis]] die before [[orsian-voldis|Orsian Voldis]] came of age, which is exactly what happened. Within a few years of Orsian's coronation, the capital nobles organized a quiet coup, stripped Aldric of his authority, and exiled him to the western borders under the pretense of military necessity.

When Orsian died heirless in 348 VR, the southern nobles answered the capital's succession claim for [[valis-voldis|Valis Voldis]] by calling for Aldric's return. That standoff, joined by anti-monarchists and reformists asking whether the House of Voldis should survive at all, is what fractured into the revolution that founded [[jesthaen|Jesthaen]].
