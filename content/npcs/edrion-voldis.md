**Summary**: High Prince of [[voldaen|Voldaen]], father of Queen [[valis-voldis|Valis Voldis]], and one of the Five Heroes who died fighting [[the-ophanim|the Ophanim]] in 334 VR, which set off the Voldis Succession Crisis.

---

Son of King [[valthis-voldis|Valthis Voldis]] and father of [[valis-voldis|Valis Voldis]]. Fought as one of the Five Heroes against [[the-ophanim|the Ophanim]] at [[hilltop|Hilltop]] in 334 VR, and died in the fighting alongside Saint Cassio of [[the-holy-see|the Holy See]].

His death was not the end of the crisis, only its start. Valthis was assassinated soon after ([[kingslayer|the Kingslayer]], see that page), leaving Edrion's young daughter [[valis-voldis|Valis]] the last heir of the direct line before she was old enough to rule. In his will, Edrion had named his bastard half-brother [[aldric-voldis|Aldric Voldis]] her regent, a provision the capital's nobles overturned by force, a choice that would go on to fracture Voldaen and birth [[jesthaen|the Republic]].
