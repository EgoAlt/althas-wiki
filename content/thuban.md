---
title: Thuban
---

**Summary**: Giant who united the survivors of Polaris's betrayal into [[draconis|Draconis]] and led it under a strict survival-first philosophy. Father of [[eltanin|Eltanin]] and [[rastaban|Rastaban]].

---

After the giants who built [[polaris|Polaris]] were turned away from the city they'd been promised a home in, and after the resource wars and desertions that followed back in the mountains, Thuban united the remaining giants into one community, [[draconis|Draconis]]. His mentality was simple: survive, the old way. Eat less, hunt less, build less, live less. It worked, on the surface.

His two sons split that philosophy down the middle. [[eltanin|Eltanin]] absorbed it completely, stoic, strong, calm, exactly like his father. [[rastaban|Rastaban]] never accepted that Polaris and its magic weren't the giants' birthright, and grew bolder rather than more resigned as he got older. When both sons turned 17, Thuban retired and passed his mantle to Eltanin; Rastaban left for Polaris the same year.

What became of Thuban afterward isn't stated.
