**Summary**: The governing institution of [[armada|Armada]]: a collection of city-state rulers who each hold their seat through possession of a physical token, not inheritance or election.

---

Armada has no single ruler. Each of its city-states is run by a member of the Guild, and members answer to no higher authority for how they govern their own territory: some rule hands-on, some barely at all, entirely up to the individual.

Guild membership isn't inherited or elected: it's held through possession of a physical token that marks its bearer as that city-state's Guild member. To become a member, you take the token from whoever currently holds it, by theft, challenge, or outright battle, however you manage it. The logic is explicitly survival-of-the-fittest: if you can hold onto the token, you've proven you deserve the seat; if someone takes it from you, you didn't. Because of this, a Guild seat changing hands by force, not succession, is simply a fact of life in Armada.
