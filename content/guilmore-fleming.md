---
title: Guilmore Fleming
---

**Summary**: Count, head of House Fleming, ruler of [[drinmery|Drinmery]]. Wants the coming war avoided and the other local houses kept neutral.

The richest and most influential lord around [[drinmery|Drinmery]], and the reason for it: House Fleming controls the city outright, owns its palaces, founded its courts, and taxes its trade. That position also gives Fleming a good read on what every other house in the area is up to, and a good reason for those houses to stay on his side.

Guilmore's priority is the status quo. He's actively working to keep the other local houses, including [[immanuel-greene|House Greene]], neutral and out of the war that's threatening to reignite.
