**Summary**: A massive lake at the geographic center of Althas, formed as a crater when [[the-one-above|the One Above]] cast [[the-ones-below|the ones below]] down at the end of the Divine Age's war.

## What it is

The lake sits at the center of the map of Althas, a crater left by the Divine Age's decisive moment: [[the-one-above|the One Above]] casting [[the-ones-below|the ones below]] down and sealing them away.
