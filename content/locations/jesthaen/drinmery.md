---
title: Drinmery
---

**Summary**: A medium-sized city in [[jesthaen|Jesthaen]], best known as the home of the [[convent-of-saint-trefan|Convent of Saint Trefan]].

---

The biggest local urban center in its stretch of [[jesthaen|Jesthaen]], and where most local trade passes through. Its most important landmark is the [[convent-of-saint-trefan|Convent of Saint Trefan]], which draws a steady stream of visitors from the church and from scholars using its library.
