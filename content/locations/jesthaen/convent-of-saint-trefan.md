**Summary**: A convent in [[drinmery|Drinmery]], home to monks and a flock of faeries, prized for its rare library.

---

Not a large convent, but the pride of its resident monks, who've built up a library with a genuinely rare and valuable book collection over the years. It draws a steady flow of visitors: church officials with business in [[drinmery|Drinmery]], and scholars coming to study.

A flock of [[faeries|faeries]] lives and works alongside the monks, using the convent's library and kitchen in exchange for service as messengers and general help around the grounds.
