**Summary**: The newest nation of Althas, a republic founded by [[aldric-voldis|Aldric Voldis]] out of the Voldis Succession Crisis and named for his mother, [[jestha|Jestha]]. The first nation of Althas to reject the divine right of the Voldis crown.

---

The newest nation of Althas, born from the Voldis Succession Crisis in [[voldaen|Voldaen]]. When the capital's noble houses drove the baseborn war-hero [[aldric-voldis|Aldric Voldis]] from the regency and ruled the realm through a child-queen, Aldric took his cause to the common people of the neglected south. The movement he built over years of exile broke into open revolution in 351 VR, rejecting the divine right of the Voldis line outright and founding a republic in its place. Aldric named it for his mother, [[jestha|Jestha]], a common woman.

Supported by [[polaris|Polaris]] and [[armada|Armada]] during the war; the Jesthaen Treaty ended active combat in 361 VR and won the Republic its recognition, though the peace with Voldaen remains tenuous. See [[index|Althas]] for the full history.
