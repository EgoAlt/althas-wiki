A federation founded by merchant lords who declared the trade cities of Hilltop's south independent during the Ophanim crisis. Home to a strong Slyborne (criminal organization) presence and looser laws than its neighbors; the one nation where Infernis are sometimes tolerated. Supported the [[jesthaen|Jesthaen]] rebels alongside [[polaris|Polaris]].

See [[althas]] for the full history.

## Government: the Guild (2026-07-12)

Armada has no single ruler. Each of its city-states is run by a member of **the Guild**, and members answer to no higher authority for how they govern their own territory, some rule hands-on, some barely at all, entirely up to the individual.

Guild membership isn't inherited or elected: it's held through possession of a physical token that marks its bearer as that city-state's Guild member. To become a member, you take the token from whoever currently holds it, by theft, by challenge, by outright battle, however you manage it. The logic is explicitly survival-of-the-fittest: if you can hold onto the token, you've proven you deserve the seat; if someone takes it from you, you didn't.
