---
title: Map
---

An interactive map of Althas. Click a marker to jump to that location's page.

```base
views:
  - type: leaflet-map
    name: Map
    image: assets/althas-map.webp
    height: 700
    minZoom: -2
    maxZoom: 2
    defaultZoom: -1
    zoomDelta: 0.25
    scale: "1"
    unit: km
```
