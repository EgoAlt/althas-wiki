title: Uriel Kenan
---

**Summary**: Player character. An Inquisitor of an oldblood [[hilltop|Hilltop]] house destroyed by [[the-ophanim|the Ophanim]], mid-pilgrimage to recover the man he was before the waters took his name.

> [!note] Player character
> Uriel Kenan is a PC, not an NPC.

Before the baptism, Uriel belonged to an oldblood elven line of [[hilltop|Hilltop]], a house where noble blood and holy office had fused so completely that no one remembered where one ended and the other began: relic-keepers, patrons, confessors, landholders, all loyal children of a faith that treated the house as part of the world's proper order.
