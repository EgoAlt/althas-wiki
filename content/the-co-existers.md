**Summary**: A cult active within [[hilltop-night-zone|the Hilltop night zone]], believing [[the-ophanim|the Ophanim]] was sent by [[the-one-above|the One Above]] to cleanse the world and prepare it for [[the-one-above|the One Above]]'s return. Named by Lucas after the co-existers from the anime *Sentenced to Be a Hero*, humans who allied with a plague of monsters trying to destroy the earth. "The Co-Existers" is a working name pending an in-fiction one.

---

## Belief

The Co-Existers hold that [[the-ophanim|the Ophanim]]'s arrival, and the permanent night it left behind, wasn't a tragedy but a mercy: [[the-one-above|the One Above]] sending the Ophanim to purge a world that had strayed, in preparation for [[the-one-above|the One Above]]'s eventual return. Where the rest of [[althas|Althas]], and the Orthodox and Reborn Cantons alike, treat the Ophanim as catastrophe, the Co-Existers treat it as the beginning of something holy.

## The parallel Lucas is drawing from

Explicitly modeled on the co-existers from the anime *Sentenced to Be a Hero*: humans who chose to side with a plague of monsters actively trying to destroy the earth, rather than resist it. The core dynamic being borrowed is the willing, ideological alliance with the thing everyone else fled from, not a forced collaboration or a Stockholm-syndrome situation, but a genuine, sincere belief that the destroyer is right and the rest of the world is wrong.

## Open questions

Not yet decided, flagged here rather than invented:

- Whether the Co-Existers have any organized structure, or leadership, or presence outside the night zone.
- What the Co-Existers actually do day to day, beyond the belief itself: worship practices, recruitment.
