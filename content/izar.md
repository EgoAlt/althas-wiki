title: Izar

**Summary**: Young mage, son of [[hesper|Hesper]], who repeatedly challenged his mother for her seat on Polaris's Triumvirate of Archmages. Publicly blamed [[rastaban|Rastaban]] for her disappearance after their final duel.

---

Challenged his mother, [[hesper|Hesper]], for her Triumvirate seat again and again, losing every time. Polaris society mostly read the ritual as Izar doing an old, stubborn woman a mercy by giving her something to win at, since she always refused to actually kill him.

The last challenge was different: Izar arrived unnaturally powerful and fast, changed by something unexplained (possibly involving his wand). He badly wounded Hesper in the fight. When [[rastaban|Rastaban]] broke Hesper's order to stay back and intervened, Hesper vanished, her house was left in shambles, and Ras lost an arm. Izar struck first, scarring Ras's eye, then immediately told anyone who'd listen that a "horrible giant" had invaded and murdered an archmage, and that he himself had struck the invader down.
