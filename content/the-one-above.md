title: "The One Above"
---

**Summary**: The god of [[splendor-magic|Splendor]], worshipped by [[the-holy-see|the Holy See]] and [[canton-of-inquisition|the Canton of Inquisition]]. Fought a war against other gods, [[the-ones-below|the ones below]], during the Divine Age; granted [[miracles|Miracles]] to allies during that war. Still alive, but has since left Althas.

---

Worshipped throughout Althas as the source of [[splendor-magic|Splendor magic]] (see that page for how incantation-based magic works as a favor granted rather than a caster's own power) and of [[miracles|Miracles]], the hereditary gifts bestowed on allies during the Divine Age's war against [[the-ones-below|the ones below]].

Unlike [[codex-magic|Codex's own patron god]], who is dead and splintered, the One Above is still alive. He has left Althas, for reasons not yet established. Whether Splendor magic and Miracles still function reliably, or at all, in his absence is an open question raised on [[splendor-magic|Splendor magic's]] own page, not yet answered.
