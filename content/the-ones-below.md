title: "The Ones Below"
---

**Summary**: The gods [[the-one-above|the One Above]] fought during the Divine Age, sealed away ("in hell, or below") since. Barely developed yet: currently only tied to [[codex-magic|Codex's]] cosmology.

---

During the Divine Age, [[the-one-above|the One Above]] fought a war against the ones below. The one thing settled so far: [[codex-magic|Codex's]] own patron god died in that conflict (or its aftermath), splintered like a Cosmere Shard, and his splintered power is what currently forms the barrier keeping the ones below sealed away. This directly ties Codex magic's source (a parallel reservoir tapped through drawn seals) to the same substance imprisoning them, though whether that's a deliberate design point or just a suggestive coincidence hasn't been confirmed.
